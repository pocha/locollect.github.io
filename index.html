Why now is the right time to build Locollect

  • UPI has become mainstream. PayTM UPI payment gateway is free to use
  • Whatsapp has opened up their APIs for business. At this moment, it is not known how much is the cost to use whatsapp APIs though.
  • UPI apps do not have great interface for auto-categorizing & filtering for easily searching past transactions.
  • No app currently provides tracking of pending payments
  • There are apps like Splitwise, which provide shared expense tracking, but their interface is clunky to use. Rather if the group responsibility is offloaded to Whatsapp, it can become much more seamless experience.

With Locollect, we want to solve the age old problem of shared expense closure. Most apps that attempted to solve the problem made it too complicated & we believe they did not get mass adoption. Indian market is fast growing & new avenues have opened up.

Locollect has the potential to become a Cibil score authority based on payment habits of folks on a more regular basis. We also intend to keep it auditable (with consent) & open/challengable so that it truely stays democratic. We intend to expose the data with the individual consent to authorities intending to give loans for a price.

Some of the other potential monetization technique could be to create paid integrations with commercial accounting softwares for pending payment reminders. Locollect can pretty much become the book keeping ledger for small business over time which can also be monetized.
